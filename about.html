About Kolisi Foundation

Founded in 2020, by Rachel and Siya Kolisi, the Kolisi Foundation was born out of a deep desire to use their platforms to drive meaningful change in our communities.

The foundation operates on the principle that every individual has the right to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for development.

Through our various programs and initiatives, we strive to create a positive impact in the lives of those we serve, fostering a sense of hope and empowerment.

Our Mission

We unite organisations to mobilise resources and strengthen infrastucture and learning. To help communities thrive, we aim to bring about systemic change through sport and education, addressing gender-based violence, and contributing to food security. We do so by partnering with organisations with a pre-existing footprint in our geographic focus areas.

Our Vision

To change the stories of inequality in South Africa, to see thriving communities.
