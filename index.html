Welcome to Kolisi Foundation

Kolisi Foundation is dedicated to making a positive impact in our community.

Get in touch with us to learn more about our initiatives.

Our Programs

  • Education & Sport: Providing resources and scholarships to underprivileged students.
  • Food Security: Addressing food insecurity through community gardens and nutrition education.
  • Gender-Based Violence: Supporting survivors and advocating for policy changes.

Learn more about our programs →

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have:

  • For Gender-Based Violence: We have supported nearly 120 survivors, and feeding nearly 600 family members.
  • We provide meals for about 4000 children daily at 6 schools in Zwide, Gqeberha.
  • We have delivered 8 projects which are libraries, community kitchens, and sports clubs.
